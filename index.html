About Us

At Dark Pattern Buster, we believe in a fair, transparent, and user-centered web experience. Our mission is to empower internet users by exposing the deceptive design techniques, known as dark patterns, that manipulate behavior and obscure choices online. With more websites and apps using sneaky tricks to pressure you into unwanted actions—whether it's subscribing to something you didn't mean to, or making it hard to unsubscribe—we decided it was time to fight back. Our extension detects and alerts you to dark patterns, giving you control over your browsing experience.
